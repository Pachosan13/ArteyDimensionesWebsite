# English SEO/AEO Keyword Research — artedim.com

Source: DataForSEO (Google Ads search volume + live SERP), July 2026.
Locations: Panama (2591) and United States (2840), language `en`.

## The honest picture

English head-term demand for architecture in Panama is **thin** (10–90/mo). This is a
low-volume, high-ticket B2B niche. Volume-chasing is the wrong strategy. Two things
make English worth doing anyway:

1. **The buyer is high-value.** Multinationals opening regional HQs, retail brands
   entering the market, and industrial operators. One conversion pays for the whole
   translation.
2. **AI Overview is already live** on `commercial architect panama`. Being the entity
   that AI engines cite is the real prize here, not blue-link volume.

## Two traps this research exposed

**Trap 1 — "Panama City" means Florida in English.** US searches for `office design panama`
and `panama city` are dominated by Panama City, FL (post office 1,300/mo, social security
office 1,600/mo). Never target bare "Panama City". Always "Panama City, **Panama**" or the
country. Schema must pin `addressCountry: PA` + geo coordinates to disambiguate the entity.

**Trap 2 — generic "architects in panama" attracts expats, not businesses.** People Also Ask
on that query returns *"Can I live on $3,000 a month in Panama?"* and *"Where do most
Americans retire in Panama?"*. That is residential/retirement intent. It does not convert
for a corporate firm. We rank for it, but we do not build the page around it.

### Generic terms — real US volume, WRONG geo. Do not chase.
`commercial architect` (3,600), `space planning` (2,900), `retail store design` (480),
`office space planning` (320), `corporate interior design` (260), `shopping mall design` (260),
`office fit out` (210), `workplace design` (210), `tenant improvement` (880),
`workplace strategy` (260). These are US-intent. Ranking for them from Panama is neither
achievable nor useful. We use them as **modifiers** (`office fit out in Panama`), never as targets.

## SERP reality — `commercial architect panama` (US, en)

Features: `ai_overview`, `local_pack` (×3), 19 organic.

Who ranks: re-thinkingthefuture (listicle "Top 40 Architects in Panama City"), grupoideaspanama,
dagarchitects (Florida!), studiodearquitectura, mallolarquitectos, melillogroup, hitopma.

**The gap:** not one Panamanian firm has a specialized English page for *corporate/commercial*
architecture. The field is listicles, aggregators (ArchDaily, Houzz, Dezeen), and an expat forum.
A real firm with real proof — 26 years, 150+ projects, 410,000+ m², named projects like Santa
María Business Park and Cemento Chagres — can take this.

`local_pack` shows on 2 of 3 queries → **Google Business Profile in English is a separate,
high-leverage action outside this repo.** Flagged for Pacho.

## AEO — questions to answer directly on-page

From People Also Ask + related searches, filtered to commercial intent:
- What does a commercial architect do in Panama?
- How much does it cost to design an office in Panama?
- How long does an office design project take in Panama?
- Can you hire an architect from another country? (→ we work with foreign clients)
- What are the top architecture firms in Panama?
- Where do multinationals locate offices in Panama? (→ Costa del Este, Panamá Pacífico)
- Do you work with companies setting up a regional headquarters in Panama?

Each of these gets a direct, self-contained answer in the first sentence, wrapped in
`FAQPage` schema. That is what gets cited.
